Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-02-26

SynapseWorks is a Jira Forge app that keeps your data scoped to your Atlassian site. We do not store Jira credentials or personal access tokens.

Data we handle

  • Issue context needed to generate BDD scenarios and create subtasks.
  • appUserToken from Atlassian Forge to authenticate actions within your site.
  • Operational logs necessary for reliability and security monitoring.

Data we do not store

  • No Jira credentials, passwords, or personal access tokens.
  • No marketing trackers or advertising cookies.
  • No data unrelated to generating BDD scenarios or creating subtasks.

Purpose of processing

Data is processed only to generate executable BDD scenarios and create linked subtasks in Jira. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

Analytics (Cloudflare Zaraz + Google Analytics 4)

Analytics is handled via Cloudflare Zaraz and Google Analytics 4. It is disabled by default and only enabled after you opt in. IP addresses are anonymized, advertising features are disabled, and signals are limited to usage metrics. You can change your choice anytime using your browser or the consent prompt when shown.

Data flow

Jira → Atlassian Forge (appUserToken) → SynapseWorks backend (processing) → Jira. Data remains within this flow for the duration of the request.

Retention

Operational logs are retained only as long as required for troubleshooting and security. Generated content and subtasks reside in your Jira site. Google Analytics event data is retained for up to 14 months and can be deleted sooner through standard GA controls.

Legal basis and consent

For visitors in the EU/EEA and UK, analytics processing is based on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time through your browser settings or by responding to the consent prompt when it appears.

Security

Access to processing services is restricted by Forge scopes and service-side controls. Sensitive tokens remain within Forge-managed context and are not persisted by SynapseWorks.

Your choices

You can update analytics consent through your browser settings or by responding to the consent prompt when it appears. You can also uninstall the app to revoke access tokens issued through Atlassian Forge. For data questions or deletion requests, contact us.
