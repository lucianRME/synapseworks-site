Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-02-26

This policy explains how synapseworks.org uses cookies and similar technologies.

How we use cookies

  • Essential storage: localStorage to remember your consent choice (accept or reject analytics).
  • Analytics: Analytics is handled via Cloudflare Zaraz and Google Analytics 4 to understand product interest and site performance. Analytics is off until you accept.

Consent choices

By default, analytics storage is set to denied and no analytics cookies are placed. Selecting "Accept analytics" in the banner enables analytics; selecting "Reject" keeps analytics off. Analytics is handled via Cloudflare Zaraz + Google Analytics 4 and is only enabled after you opt in. You can change your choice anytime using your browser or the consent prompt when shown.

Google Analytics 4

  • Events are recorded only after consent.
  • IP anonymization is enabled; advertising features and ad personalization signals are disabled.
  • Event data retention is up to 14 months, managed within GA4.

Managing preferences

You can change or withdraw consent at any time using your browser or the consent prompt when shown. Your preference is stored so we can honor it on future visits.
